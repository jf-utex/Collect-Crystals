Crystals Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There will be four crystals. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific number of points to your score.


You will win the game by matching your total score to the random number provided, you lose the game if your total score goes above the target random number.


The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.


Each time the game starts, the value of the crystals will be randomly reassigned.
