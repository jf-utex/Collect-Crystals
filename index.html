Crystals Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four crystals to choose from each assigned a ramdom number that will be revealed after selecting that crystal. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific number of points to your score.


You will win the game by matching your total score to the random number provided, you lose the game if your total score is above the target random number.


Each time the game starts, the value of the crystals will be randomly reassigned.
